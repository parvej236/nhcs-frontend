# Complete Doctor Journey and Workflow

## Scenario

Dr. Ahmed is a registered cardiologist working at a hospital that is connected to the National Unified Digital Healthcare Ecosystem.

This document describes the complete end-to-end workflow from a doctor's perspective, including patient management, treatment creation, report review, follow-ups, clinical decision support, and long-term patient care.

---

# Phase 1: Doctor Registration and Verification

## Initial Registration

A doctor enters the platform through:

/doctor

The doctor submits:

* National ID
* BMDC Registration Number
* Medical Degree Information
* Specialization Information
* Workplace Information
* Contact Information

---

## Authority Verification

The hospital authority verifies:

* Identity
* BMDC License
* Educational Credentials
* Employment Status

Government authority may additionally verify:

* National medical registry
* License validity
* Disciplinary records

---

## Doctor Account Activation

After approval:

The system creates:

* Doctor ID
* Professional Profile
* Digital Signature
* Clinical Workspace
* Patient Management Dashboard

The doctor can now provide treatment through the ecosystem.

---

---

# Ultimate Goal

The doctor should never need to:

* Ask repeatedly for old reports
* Read hundreds of pages manually
* Search multiple hospital systems
* Depend on paper prescriptions
* Reconstruct patient history from memory

Instead, every patient interaction becomes a continuation of a complete lifelong digital medical record, allowing doctors to make faster, safer, and more informed clinical decisions.
